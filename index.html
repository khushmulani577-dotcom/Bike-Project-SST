Chapter 01

Introduction

Khalnayak Bikes is a custom motorcycle business inspired by bold and powerful designs. The theme of the brand is based on a strong villain personality inspired by Sanjay Dutt. These bikes are designed to look aggressive, stylish, and unique compared to normal motorcycles.

The business focuses on manufacturing high-quality custom bikes similar to Harley-Davidson but at a more affordable price. The aim is to provide a combination of performance, comfort, and attractive design. These bikes represent personality and attitude.
